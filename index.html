Our Mission

Clearview Sales Company was founded in Iowa with a focus on people building dependable teams that work hard, stay honest, and deliver results. Led by Michael Purdy, weve grown from a small local operation into a company that trains and supports traveling sales teams across the Midwest. Our goal is simple: create opportunity for motivated individuals while helping our partners reach new customers. We handle the travel, training, and logistics so our reps can focus on doing their job well meeting people, earning fairly, and representing strong values wherever they go.
